About Pormat

Making Your Real Estate Dreams Real

Pormat Property Group traces back to 2015, when its founders launched their first development — a block of flats in Pretoria North — and ended up marketing and letting the units themselves after struggling to find an agency that could. That hands-on experience uncovered a gap in the market and grew into what Pormat is today.

The company is built around helping property owners market and manage their own projects, backed by a team with two decades of combined real estate experience and full PPRA registration. This portal gives our clients direct, up-to-date access to what's currently available, without the back-and-forth of PDFs and email threads.
